The Campfire is a place to have fun and connect, so please follow the 3 simple R's:

Respect:

  

Respect your brothers and sisters – don't be offensive, abusive, discriminate or be racist at the Campfire.

Represent:

  

The Campfire is about adding your own unique spark – represent! Don't post something you haven't personally created or impersonate anyone else.

Respond:

  

Help NITV keep the Campfire family-friendly by flagging anything you think may be inappropriate.
